## 2. Cash-yield convention (frozen)

- Rate source: **FRED DTB3** (3-month T-bill secondary market, daily,
  re-downloadable, public CSV endpoint). PIT: the rate applied on day t is
  the last published value as of **t−2** (publication-lag conservative);
  weekends/holidays carry forward.
- Accrual: `yield(t) = max(rate, 0)/365 × idle(t)`, with
  `idle(t) = 1 − max(gross(t−1), gross(t))` (redeployment-lag conservative:
  cash entering or leaving deployment earns nothing that day).
- Never promotional/lending APYs. Reporting: **both** Sharpe conventions
  (rf=0 total-return AND excess-of-cash) for every series from now on.
- Yield adoption is **unconditional** (it is accounting, not risk); the VT20
  adoption rule is below.
